SimTrader

Overview

SimTrader is a browser based market trading simulation game.

It uses real historical market data to provide a simulated trading environment.

The market is displayed as 1 minute Candlestick Chart.

Market Volume is displayed as a series of bars along the bottom of the chart.

Clock

After SimTrader finished loading, click the 'Ready!' button to start the simulation.

Click the clock while it is running to pause the simulation.

Click it again to resume the simulation.

You can change the speed of the simulation to run faster or slower.

Use the left arrow button, located under the clock, to slow the simulation.

Use the right arrow button, located under the clock, to speed up the simulation.

Trading

While the simulation is running, you can buy and sell the market.

The green right arrow button will buy a single unit.

The red left arrow button will sell a single unit.

The yellow button will flatten your position.

Buy when the price of the market is low and sell when the market price is high.
